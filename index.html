Our Story

Built by the Trade.
Scaled the Right Way.

Affiliate Warehouse was created alongside its sister company, Aussie Antennas and Electrical, after more than 25 years of working with trusted contractors across Australia.

Aussie Antennas and Electrical grew nationally by partnering with preferred contractors — not by owning trucks in every city. The model worked. But as the work scaled, the purchasing didn't.

Every contractor bought materials through their own trade accounts, paying small-business pricing with inconsistent discounts and layered mark-ups. National-level spending was being handled like hundreds of separate businesses.

Affiliate Warehouse was built as a separate, independent company to fix that — one shared purchasing system, corporate-level supplier pricing, used by our preferred contractors nationwide. Same independence. Better pricing. A system that finally matches the scale of the work.

Collective Buying

How Volume Creates Better Pricing

Independent preferred contractors, rather than purchasing separately through individual accounts, may purchase through our national corporate accounts. Aggregated purchasing volumes may enable access to pricing structures typically available to larger operators — subject to supplier approval and terms.

This benefits both contractors — through improved pricing visibility — and suppliers, through greater volume consistency and narrowed purchasing across fewer accounts nationally.

Legal Disclaimer

Pricing and discounts are set by wholesalers and subject to change at any time. Affiliate Warehouse does not guarantee savings on all products or purchases. Larger or high-volume items typically see stronger results; savings vary by product, supplier, and prevailing market conditions. Fees apply only where verifiable savings occur, calculated monthly on confirmed discounts. Participation is non-exclusive and entirely voluntary. Affiliate Warehouse operates as a procurement support and price-monitoring service only — not as a reseller or distributor. All purchasing decisions remain with the contractor. Access to shared accounts is subject to formal application review and approval; access is never automatic and may be refused at the discretion of Affiliate Warehouse.
